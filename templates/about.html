About Health Care Center

AI-Powered Symptom Checker for Awareness and Guidance

Our Purpose

Health Care Center is an AI-based web application designed to help users understand their symptoms and get an idea of possible health conditions. It uses a trained Machine Learning model to analyze user inputs and suggest likely diseases with basic guidance.

  • Predicts possible diseases based on symptoms
  • Shows a short description of the condition
  • Suggests basic precautions and medications (informational only)
  • Recommends related workouts and diets

Note: This tool is for educational use only — not a medical diagnosis substitute.

How It Works
  1. User enters or speaks their symptoms.
  2. The system encodes symptoms into numeric form.
  3. A trained ML model (Support Vector Classifier) predicts the disease.
  4. The app displays the result along with precautions and advice.
Who Can Use It?
  • Students learning AI and healthcare integration
  • Developers experimenting with Flask + ML
  • People exploring basic AI-based symptom checkers
Responsible Use

Predictions are based on existing datasets and are not 100% accurate. Always consult a doctor for any medical issue or emergency.

Future Plans
  • Better training datasets for higher accuracy
  • Support for multiple languages
  • Integration with verified healthcare APIs
  • Personalized symptom tracking and reports
